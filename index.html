About Me

My name is Yiftach Brown.
I am 37 years old and currently live in Petah Tikva, Israel.
I am divorced and the father of two lovely and sweet girls, 5 and 3 years old.

Education

Has a bachelor's degree in political science,
communication and orientalism from Ariel University.
Has a teaching certificate in teaching Israeli history
and philosophy from David Yelin College.

Occupation

Educator, teacher and presenter for matriculation in mathematics
for grades 9-12, expert in the "Step" method for learning mathematics
for those who have difficulty.
In addition, I serves as an operational director in the high school,
a social coordinator and a disciplinary coordinator.

Hobbies

Playing different ball games and watching them, playing the clarinet,
creating music, writing a Torah thought, studying Torah.
